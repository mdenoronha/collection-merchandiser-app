Instructions

The Collection Merchandiser app helps you identify low and out of stock products to help better merchande your collections. Getting started is easy.

Select a Collection

Search for the collection you wish to sort by title.

Note: you will only be able to sort products on collections that are sorted manually. Update the sort method if you you need to make that change.

Select Sort for quick sorting, select Sort (with Variants) for slower sorting with variants.

Select your Options

Use the 'Rows' selector to choose how many products per row you'd like to view in the app. Use the display checkboxes to show or hide product information.

Use the remaining options to show and hide product information you'd like to see. The 'Availability' option refers to whether the product is visible on the online store

Sort Products

Sort products by dragging and dropping them in a preferred order. You can select multiple product by clicking on the required products and dragging them to the preferred place.

Once completed, just click 'Save' and you're done.

Quick Sort

Use the quick sort feature to sort the products based on particularly criteria (e.g. stock levels, recency). For large collections, allow a few seconds for all products to be loaded and sorted

Once it's done, don't forget to hit Save to complete your changes.
